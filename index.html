Join OTU E-Riders

Become a part of the electric mobility revolution with OTU E-Riders, where we explore the future of transportation - micromobility! Our ratified student club is all about electric scooters, e-bikes, and personal electric vehicles (PEVs). Join us to ride, learn, and meet fellow enthusiasts.
