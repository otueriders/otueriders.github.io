About

Welcome to the OTU E-Riders blog, your go-to source for all things electric scooters, e-bikes, and personal electric vehicles (PEVs). Our blog is where we share our passion for micromobility and sustainable transportation. Whether you're an enthusiast, a beginner, or just curious, our blog has something for you. Our team of student writers curate content that covers a wide range of topics, including electric scooter reviews, maintenance tips, the latest trends in electric mobility, and much more.
