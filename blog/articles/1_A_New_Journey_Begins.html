Welcome to the inaugural blog post of "The Scooter Chronicles," your ultimate source for all things electric mobility and the official blog of the Ontario Tech University E-Riders Club!

We are thrilled to kick off this exciting journey with a significant milestone - the ratification of our club by the Ontario Tech Student Union. It's a momentous occasion that marks the beginning of a new era of electric scooter and e-bike enthusiasts coming together to promote sustainable transportation on our campus.

What Is the Ontario Tech E-Riders Club?

For those new to the scene, the Ontario Tech E-Riders Club, lovingly known as OTU E-Riders, is a passionate community of students who share a common interest in electric scooters, e-bikes, and personal electric vehicles (PEVs). We believe in the power of micromobility to transform the way we commute and navigate our beautiful campus and city.

Ratification: The Gateway to New Possibilities

Obtaining ratification from the Ontario Tech Student Union is a significant achievement for our club. It opens doors to a world of possibilities and resources that will help us thrive as a community. Here's what ratification means for us:

  • Official Recognition: With ratification, we are now officially recognized as a student club at Ontario Tech University. This recognition comes with the support and endorsement of the student union, reinforcing our club's legitimacy and importance.
  • Access to Funding: Ratification grants us access to funding opportunities and resources provided by the student union. These resources will be instrumental in organizing events, workshops, and activities that promote electric mobility on campus.
  • Collaborative Opportunities: Being a ratified club allows us to collaborate with other student clubs and organizations within the university. We're excited about the potential partnerships and initiatives that will help us expand our reach and impact.
  • Community Growth: With the student union's backing, we expect our community of electric mobility enthusiasts to grow rapidly. We welcome new members who share our passion and curiosity about the world of electric scooters, e-bikes, and PEVs.
