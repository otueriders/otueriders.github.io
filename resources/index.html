Resources

Here are some valuable resources for electric scooters and personal electric vehicles (PEV's):

  • Rider Guide - A comprehensive guide to electric scooters and e-bikes.
  • Electrek - News, reviews, and updates on electric vehicles, including scooters and e-bikes.
  • Stable Micromobility Innovative solutions for micromobility enthusiasts addressing PEV theft and working towards micromobility innovation.
  • Reddit's Electric Scooters Subreddit - A Reddit community dedicated to electric scooters, where you can find discussions and recommendations.
  • Scooter Talk Forum - An online community for electric scooter enthusiasts to discuss and share experiences.
